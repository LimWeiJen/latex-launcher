\section{Changing Metadata}

To change the assignment details such as the course code, course name, assignment title, and your personal details, simply open \texttt{main.tex} and locate the \textbf{USER METADATA CONFIGURATION} section at the top. 

Modify the following commands with your own details:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \verb|\newcommand{\mycoursecode}{YOUR COURSE CODE}|
    \item \verb|\newcommand{\mycoursename}{YOUR COURSE NAME}|
    \item \verb|\newcommand{\myassignmenttitle}{YOUR ASSIGNMENT TITLE}|
    \item \verb|\newcommand{\mystudentname}{YOUR NAME}|
    \item \verb|\newcommand{\mymatricno}{YOUR MATRIC NUMBER}|
    \item \verb|\newcommand{\mycourselecturer}{YOUR LECTURER}|
\end{itemize}

These variables will automatically populate the cover page, headers and footers, and the PDF metadata.
